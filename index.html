Privacy first

Your health data is yours.

Your health data will always remain yours, and will never be shared without your consent. OxynX is committed to the highest privacy standards, with HIPAA-aligned safeguards in place to ensure safe handling of protected health information. OxynX will never sell the personal data of its users.

  • Encrypted in transit over HTTPS/TLS, with access controls and audit logging.
  • No selling of data. OxynX will never sell your personal data.
  • De-identified data. Only de-identified data is used for analytics and app improvements.
  • Delete anytime. Request to delete your account and data at any point.
