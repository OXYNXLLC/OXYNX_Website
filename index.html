Built private

Your health data is yours. We protect it like it.

OxynX is a privacy-first health platform with HIPAA-aligned safeguards and contractual controls designed to support compliant handling of protected health information. We don’t sell personal data or health data — ever.

  • Encrypted in transit over HTTPS/TLS, with access controls and audit logging.
  • No selling of data. We don’t use health data for advertising personalization.
  • De-identified only with consent for safety, analytics, and privacy-first improvement.
  • Delete anytime. Request account and data deletion whenever you choose.
